Bhante Sujato’s translation essays

2018-03-23T10:29:49.019Z

Original: https://discourse.suttacentral.net/t/bhante-sujato-s-translation-essays/8867

During the course of my translation work on the 4 nikāyas, I wrote around 130 essays on various points that came up in the translation. These vary greatly in nature, but most address little details and unusual points of interest. And hopefully they will show something of the methods and approaches I used in my work.

These essays are collected here on Discourse under the tag ebt-translation . This tag also includes a few related essays by Vens Brahmali and Vimala.
